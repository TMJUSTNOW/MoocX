## Prices

Prices are expressed with letters in the following way in German:

|            | German/Deutsch        | English/Englisch |
| ---------- | --------------------- | ---------------- |
| **0,05 €** | fünf Cent             | five cents       |
| **2 €**    | zwei Euro             | two Euros        |
| **2,10 €** | zwei Euro zehn (Cent) | two Euro ten     |
