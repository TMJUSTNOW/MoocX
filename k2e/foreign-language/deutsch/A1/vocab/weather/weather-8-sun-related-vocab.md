## Sun related Vocab



- sonnenbrand - sun burn
- sonnencreme - sunscreen
- sonnenbrille - sun glasses
- sonnenhut - a hat specifically for protection from sun
- sonnenschirm - parasol
- sonnenblume - sunflower
- sonnenstich - sunstroke
